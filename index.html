DC Circuits Via the 7E Learning Model

About

This learning material is designed according to the 7E learning model with the integration of computer simulations in the different learning activities of students. The simulations used are from the PhET Interactive Simulations of the University of Colorado Boulder which can be freely used and/or redistributed by third parties for non-commercial or commercial purposes. The set of activities are designed for STEM students of the senior high school curriculum regardless of the type and classification of these schools. The target competencies are stated in the lesson exemplar that can be accessed through the teachers’ resource corner.
